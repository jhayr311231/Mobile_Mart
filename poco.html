POCO X3 SPECIFICATIONS

₱10,889


The POCO X3 Pro is a "bang-for-the-buck" smartphone that features a 6.67-inch FHD+ display with a 120Hz screen refresh rate, a 48MP + 8MP + 2MP + 2MP quad rear camera matrix,
and a single 20MP punch-hole selfie camera
It runs on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 860 chipset with a 2.96GHz octa-core CPU, LiquidCool technology for heat dissipation, either 6GB or 8GB of RAM, and an Android 11-based MIUI 12 software.
There's also a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, 4G LTE connectivity, NFC, IR blaster, Hi-Res audio, and expandable storage.
A massive 5160mAh battery powers the device with support for 33W fast charging technology.
The official price of the POCO X3 Pro in the Philippines is ₱11,990.00 for the (6GB RAM + 128GB storage) version
(introductory price of ₱9,990.00 on March 24, 2021) and ₱14,990.00 for the (8GB RAM + 256GB storage) version (introductory price of ₱10,990.00 on March 24, 2022).
